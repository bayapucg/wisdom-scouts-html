About Nice Scouts Guides

Nice Scouts Guides is a non-profit, non-political and secular organization. NSGI is a movement that aims to support young people in their physical, mental, social and within spiritual development, that they may play constructive roles in society,with a strong focus on the outdoors and survival skills.
