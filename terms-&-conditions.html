Terms & Conditions

Rules and policies are very important for all of us. We all have to work. But how can we do so if we don’t know how to do so. So we prepare and follow some rules and regulations to make our work easy and proper. In Nice Scouts Guides(India) Rules and regulations are the most important factors. Discipline, Punctuality, hard work, Patriotism and brotherhood are main aspects of Nice Scouts Guides(India). And all these aspects play an important role in creating a better world.

Note : please go through the rules and policies carefully before joining the Nice Scouts Guides.

1. Nice Scouts Guides(India) is world wide movement and everyone is always welcomed to join and to know more about Scouts Guides. For membership one has to apply online and has to submit the fixed DD of Rs 60 k. at the time of receiving the membership which is totally non refundable.

2. Nice Scouts Guides(India) is working for PAN India. So is always in need of employees who can give their services to the Nation on the basis of Nice Scouts Guides(India). To apply for job one has to go through the site and fill the online form to register. The registration fees is Rs 550/- the applicant will be provided with the proper receipt of fees. Applicant has to be very careful while applying,if the form is found to be incomplete, any error or with any wrong information then the form will be rejected.

3. Then the applicant will be called for Written examination. Once written is qualified the applicant is eligible for interview. After qualifying the Interview, applicant has to go through the Medical Test. And after this applicant going to Documents Verification.

4. When the applicant Selected For the Training then he has to pay the DD of Rs 60k. Non - refundable and will become the employee of Nice Scouts Guides(India). There are no other hidden charges other than this.

5. HE/She has to submit the fixed DD of Rs 60 k. at the time of receiving the Training Letter. Which is totally Non-Refundable. Candidate Submit these charge for Training,accomadation, food and organisation development.

6. After the selection, the candidate is provided with the training of maximum one month, then will be deployed(as per offer letter). It will take 90 days to be deployed after training.

7. Consumption of alcohol, any other kind of intoxication is strictly prohibited. if anyone is found doing so will be terminated without any prior notice.

8. If someone from Nice Scouts Guides is found guilty for any kind of bribe or illegal transaction, strict action will be taken against him/her by Nice Scouts Guides(India) and will be taken legally according to IPC of India.

9. Nice Scouts Guides(India) is not responsible for any kind of transaction other than the registration fee and Demand draft. (as mentioned above) and receipt will be provided for the amount paid

10. The Provisional period of trainee under Nice Scouts Guides(India) is from Six months. And the Probation period will be of one years. The Trainee will avail all the facilities and benefits of Nice Scouts Guides(India) after the completion of probation period.

11. In the provisional period the trainee will be paid only for the working days. The trainee will not be paid for his personal or school offs.

12. The promotion of the candidate will be done on the basis of their performance, remarks from the School or report submitted by the senior.

13. No one in the Organization while working, whether employee, member or anyone has no right to go against the organisation without any specific reason.

14. Consumption of alcohol, any misconduct, abusive language, misbehave, arguments with senior, Smoking, during the duty hours or training hours is strictly prohibited. If someone is found doing so, Strict actions will be taken against him/her or can be terminated immediately without any prior notice.

15. All the empoyees, members or anyone associated with Nice Scouts Guides(India), has to inform atleast one month before leaving the organization, leaving without informing will led them to pay the panelty or fine depending on the losses of the Organization which are occured due to the accused person.
